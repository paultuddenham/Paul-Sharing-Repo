Borough Market

London's best food market — graze as you wander. Bread Ahead doughnuts, Kappacasein toasted cheese, Monmouth coffee. Southwark Cathedral next door is a lovely, calm 5-min detour.

🛍️ For Phoebe
  • Bermondsey Street (10-min walk south) — indie boutiques, the White Cube gallery & lovely little homeware shops.
  • Market makers' stalls — ceramics, olive wood, spices & artisan treats to take home.
🍺 For Dad & Sis
  • The Market Porter — proper old market pub, real ales, been pouring since dawn for the traders.
  • The George Inn (Borough High St) — London's last galleried coaching inn (National Trust); pints in the historic courtyard.
  • Market bites: a Scotch egg, a hot salt-beef roll, or fresh oysters by the river.
🗺️ Borough Market🍺 Market Porter

Covent Garden

The Piazza street performers are a hit with Phoebe & Alfie. Lunch — proper classic London institutions:

  • ⭐ Rules (Maiden Lane) — London's oldest restaurant (1798), traditional British (game, pies, puddings), all velvet & history. You've been & loved it. Best to book for Sat lunch.
  • J Sheekey (St Martin's Court) — the classic 1896 theatreland seafood institution (famous fish pie, oysters); somewhere new in the same vein.
  • Rock & Sole Plaice (Endell St) — London's oldest chippy (1871), the casual, kid-easy classic if you fancy proper fish & chips.

Gelato at Venchi after.

🛍️ For Phoebe
  • Seven Dials & Neal's Yard — the prettiest corner: Aesop, Le Labo, Neal's Yard Remedies, indie fashion.
  • The Apple Market — handmade jewellery & craft stalls under the glass roof.
  • Charlotte Tilbury, Space NK & Chanel Beauty around the Piazza; the pretty Moomin & Whittard shops too.
🍺 For Dad & Sis
  • The Lamb & Flag (Rose St) — a gloriously old, wonky Covent Garden pub tucked down an alley.
  • The Harp (Chandos Pl) — multi-award-winning ale house, famous for its sausages.
⭐ Rules🍽️ J Sheekey🐟 Rock & Sole🛍️ Neal's Yard

Liberty + Carnaby

Liberty is worth it just to wander — the mock-Tudor building is stunning. Carnaby & Kingly Court next door for more shops, coffee, loos & high chairs.

🛍️ For Phoebe
  • Liberty Beauty Hall — the famous one; plus Liberty's signature scarves & fabric prints.
  • Carnaby: & Other Stories, COS, Ganni, Jo Malone, Lush, and Kingly Court's little boutiques.
🍺 For Dad & Sis
  • BrewDog Soho or a proper Soho boozer — the Clachan (Kingly St) is a beautiful old Victorian pub right by Liberty.
🗺️ Liberty🛍️ Carnaby☕ Kingly Court

16:45

Camden

Buzzing markets, the Lock & the canal. Global street food for a late graze. Calmer with the kids? The canal towpath up to Primrose Hill is beautiful (and great views).

🛍️ For Phoebe
  • Camden Market & the Stables — vintage, indie designers, leather, jewellery & one-off finds.
  • Fun with Alfie: the weird & wonderful Cyberdog.
🍺 For Dad & Sis
  • The Hawley Arms — the legendary Camden pub (Amy Winehouse's local); great terrace.
  • BrewDog Camden or a canal-side pint at The Lock Tavern.
🗺️ Camden Market🍺 Hawley Arms🌳 Primrose Hill
