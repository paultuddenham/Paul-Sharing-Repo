Borough Market

London's best food market — graze as you wander. Bread Ahead doughnuts, Kappacasein toasted cheese, Monmouth coffee. Southwark Cathedral next door is a lovely, calm detour.

🛍️ For Phoebe tap
  • Bermondsey Street (10-min walk south) — indie boutiques, the White Cube gallery & lovely homeware shops.
  • The market's maker stalls — ceramics, olive wood, spices & artisan treats to take home.
🍺 For Dad & Sis tap
  • The Market Porter — proper old market pub, real ales, pouring since dawn for the traders.
  • The George Inn — London's last galleried coaching inn (National Trust); pints in the historic courtyard.
  • Market bites: a Scotch egg, a hot salt-beef roll, or fresh oysters by the river.

Covent Garden

The Piazza street performers are a hit with Phoebe & Alfie. Lunch — a few classic London institutions to pick from:

  • ⭐ The Delaunay (Aldwych) — a grand, old-world café-restaurant (Wolseley team); that Rules-era character but all-day & relaxed with the kids.
  • J Sheekey (St Martin's Court) — the 1896 theatreland seafood institution; wood panelling, actor photos, famous fish pie & oysters.
  • Rock & Sole Plaice (Endell St) — London's oldest chippy (1871); casual & kid-easy.

Gelato at Venchi after.

🛍️ For Phoebe tap
  • Seven Dials & Neal's Yard — the prettiest corner: Aesop, Le Labo, Neal's Yard Remedies, indie fashion.
  • The Apple Market — handmade jewellery & craft stalls under the glass roof.
  • Charlotte Tilbury, Space NK & Chanel Beauty around the Piazza; the pretty Moomin & Whittard shops too.
🍺 For Dad & Sis tap
  • The Lamb & Flag (Rose St) — a gloriously old, wonky pub tucked down an alley.
  • The Harp (Chandos Pl) — multi-award-winning ale house, famous for its sausages.

Liberty + Carnaby

Liberty is worth it just to wander — the mock-Tudor building is stunning. Carnaby & Kingly Court next door for more shops, coffee, loos & high chairs.

🛍️ For Phoebe tap
  • Liberty Beauty Hall — the famous one; plus Liberty's signature scarves & fabric prints.
  • Carnaby: & Other Stories, COS, Ganni, Jo Malone, Lush, and Kingly Court's little boutiques.
🍺 For Dad & Sis tap
  • The Clachan (Kingly St) — a beautiful old Victorian pub right by Liberty. Or BrewDog Soho round the corner.

Camden

Buzzing markets, the Lock & the canal. Global street food for a late graze. Calmer with the kids? The canal towpath up to Primrose Hill is beautiful (great views).

🛍️ For Phoebe tap
  • Rokit — the best vintage in Camden: four floors of curated designer & vintage (20s–90s), plus their own reworked pieces.
  • Collectif — gorgeous 40s/50s retro glamour: pin-up dresses & vintage-style womenswear.
  • The Vintage Collection (Saddle Row) — one of the UK's largest unworn-vintage collections, 30s–90s.
  • Camden Market & the Stables — indie designers, leather & jewellery. (Fun with Alfie: the weird & wonderful Cyberdog.)
🍺 For Dad & Sis tap
  • The Hawley Arms — the legendary Camden pub (Amy Winehouse's local); great terrace.
  • BrewDog Camden, or a canal-side pint at The Lock Tavern.
